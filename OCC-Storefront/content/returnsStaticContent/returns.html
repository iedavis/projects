Returns

Good news!

We understand that you may change your mind. So to give you peace of mind, CloudLake.com offers you 30 days after the delivery date of your product to return it, free of charge.

How do I return my item?

  1. Complete the returns form you received with your order.
  2. Pack the completed form with your item(s), if possible in the original packaging provided.
  3. Attach the prepaid returns slip to the packaged item(s) and call DHL on 0825 10 00 00 to schedule a collection time.
    4. The prepaid returns slip is contained within the plastic sleeve attached to your order. Don't worry, if you've lost the label just contact us requesting a new one.
  4. You'll receive a confirmation email that we have received your parcel at our warehouse. Upon receipt, we will check the item(s).
  5. You'll receive a second confirmation email confirming your refund.
