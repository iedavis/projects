CloudLake's Cookie Policy

What are Cookies

A cookie is a piece of information in the form of a very small text file which is stored on your computer when you visit certain web pages. A cookie can be thought of as an internet user's identification card, which tells a website when the user has returned. Cookies can't harm your computer.

Your browser accesses the cookie file only when you visit the website that generated it. This helps to ease your navigation by automatically logging you in and remembering things like your preferences and what's in your shopping basket. Cookies allow sites like CloudLake.com to deliver you a personalised shopping experience.

To order products on CloudLake.com, you need to have cookies enabled. If you don't wish to enable cookies, you'll still be able to browse the site although you won’t be able to make any purchases online.

You can find out more about the various types of cookie here.
